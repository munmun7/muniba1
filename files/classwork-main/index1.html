Coffee is a brewed drink made from the roasted seeds of several species of an evergreen shrub of the genus Coffea, namely Coffea arabica and Coffea canephora. English word coffee comes from Dutch koffie, which came from Turkish kahve, which is, in turn, borrowed from Arabic qahwa, which is a short form of qahhwat al-bun which means “wine of the bean”. There is also theory that Turkish kahve comes from Arabic qahā, which means "to have no appetite" because it was believed that this beverage dulls hunger.
